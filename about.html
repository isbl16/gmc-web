About GMC

After working for a number of Non-Government Organisations (NGO's) for the past 28 years, Grace has decided to establish a consulting and counselling service for men and their families where men, young and mature can be heard, understood, without being judged, but supported, and nurtured. With her extensive clinical experience Grace has helped men and families in Queensland. For Grace, no case is too small, simple or complex. Many men has entrusted her their innermost struggle over relationships with their partner, parent/s, children, siblings and colleagues. Grace has also helped men and families with issues regarding finances, career, health and well-being. These men are Great Men. Great men seek help when experiencing personal crisis and take responsibility for their actions. Great men make time for consultation and receive counsel at GMC where leaders and positive role models are made. At GMC, men are equipped with healthy beliefs, values, realistic expectations, and an open-mind necessary to keep his love ones safe and nurtured. Great men seek counselling to be part of a solution and stop being part of a problem.

Greatness at GMC is about being prepared to consult an experienced counsellor like Grace and her team. The team will take good care of you and tackle your personal issues. Your love ones will soon notice the 'new' you.

Greatness at GMC means looking after yourself emotionally, physically, socially, intellectually, financially and spiritually so people around you can feel the positive energy that your 'new self' projects.

Grace understands men and has worked with men from all walks of life. She brings inspiration into your life and helps you become great again through counselling. She helps you restore a healthy relationship so you stay connected with your love ones. Grace travels on a regular basis to the USA/UK/Europe and ASIA to observe, meet and speak with men and families to gain a global perspectives-a testament that GMC understands men!

Grace's clinical experience spans 28 years. She's seen it all. You know you're in good hands because she is patient, supportive, non-judgemental, and will tell you everything you need to know about your issues. Her aim is to give you a better understanding of what is happening in your life. The GMC assessment provides the catalyst for your renewed interest and curiosity. The GMC strategies are the impetus for change bringing you a brighter, calmer, stable, manageable, responsible and a balance outlook in life.

Grace holds a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology and a Master of Applied Law from the University of Queensland (UQ).
