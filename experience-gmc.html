Experience GMC

Why GMC?

  • The Great Men Consultancy is a man and family oriented consulting and counselling support service. The GMC has worked with men and families from all walks of life
  • GMC understands men, their perspectives, processes, and potential
  • GMC is a man's partner in keeping his family and love ones emotionally, psychologically and physically safe when you are experiencing personal crisis
  • The GMC is man's best friend
  • GMC delivers measurable outcome
  • Our strategies are practical, portable and age-appropriate-they are your skills for life
  • At GMC we go deeper and wider in our overall assessment giving a realistic picture so you gain an understanding of your situation. Based on our assessment, we aim to work on the changes necessary to achieve a brighter future for you and your love ones.
  • Importantly, GMC is discreet, private and confidential
  • Whether you have your own psychiatrist, psychologist, legal adviser and other professional help, GMC will be there to stand by you.
  • Choose us to protect, nurture and care for you and your love ones from unintended emotional and psychological effect resulting from one's personal crisis.
  • Grace travels regularly to USA/UK/Europe and Asia to observe and talk to men and families in order to gain a global perspective on men, families and relationships, in general.
  • GMC is serious about the business of relationships and support for men and families so you have a healthy, strong and safe relationship
  • GMC is the name you can trust with 28 years of solid clinical experience working with men and families. We look after you and your love ones like our own.
  • We provide Face-to Face consultation and counselling.
  • GMC consulting and counselling is about entrusting us issues you need help with.
  • We do the extra mile to rebuild your life
  • GMC relieves you of your stress and concern about who is going to look after your love ones while you're temporarily struggling due to personal crisis
  • Let the highly experienced therapist of GMC assist you in difficult times
  • We pride ourselves of our in-depth assessment that is followed with practical and effective strategies.
  • GMC delivers measurable outcome
  • We pride ourselves with our professional and nurturing approach.
  • With clinical experience that spans 28 years we are highly experience to deal with any complex case and situation.
  • We are part of your solution.
  • Need support and nurturing for you and your family today? Call GMC now!

  • We specialise in men and families
  • We respond to a man's call for help in times of personal crisis by looking after his family's wellbeing by putting in place a system that prioritises the stabilization of conflict, responds to provide a nurturing, trusting, calm, and safe environment. It aims to maintain connection, early resolution, minimize disruption and healing for those who are affected
